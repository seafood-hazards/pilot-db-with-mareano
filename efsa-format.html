EFSA Format

Data format of the EFDA reporting tool on trace elements

The page presents the the data format of the EFDA reporting tool necessary for submitting the background sediment data.

Report on Trace Elements

The details of EFSA submission and their objectives are described in this call from EFSA.

  • Data on natural background concentrations of trace elements (cobalt, copper, iodine, manganese, molybdenum, selenium, and zinc)
  • Trace element concentrations in marine sediment along the coasts of EU, EFTA, and EU candidate countries (including fjords and estuaries)
  • Samples collected within the last 15 years (2009 to present)
  • Samples from pristine areas (defined as areas unaffected by point or diffuse pollution sources)
  • Supporting metadata regarding porewater pH, sediment texture (% clay <2 µm, % silt 2–63 µm, % sand >63 µm), and organic matter content (%)
